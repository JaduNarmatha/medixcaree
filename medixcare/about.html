History and Milestones

Founded over two decades ago, our hospital has grown from a small clinic into a multi-specialty facility. Key milestones include the opening of our advanced cardiac unit, multiple successful surgeries, and collaborations with top international healthcare providers, making us a trusted name in the medical field.

Leadership and Team

Our experienced leadership team is dedicated to guiding the hospital toward continuous improvement and innovation. Led by our CEO and supported by top medical specialists, we take pride in fostering a culture of excellence and collaboration to deliver the best patient outcomes.

Green Initiatives

We are committed to sustainability through eco-friendly practices, energy-efficient equipment, and waste management programs. Our hospital building is designed to reduce environmental impact, using solar energy and water recycling systems, while maintaining a healing atmosphere for our patients and staff.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Our hospital is equipped with cutting-edge diagnostic tools, advanced surgical theaters, and specialized treatment centers. From robotic-assisted surgeries to minimally invasive procedures, we invest in the latest medical technologies to offer high-quality healthcare services to our patients.

Specialized Services

We offer a wide range of specialized services, including cardiology, oncology, orthopedics, maternity care, and pediatric services. Our expert medical teams are dedicated to providing tailored treatment plans that meet the unique needs of each patient, ensuring the best possible care.

Awards and Recognitions

Our hospital has received numerous awards for excellence in healthcare, patient satisfaction, and innovation in medical practices. We are proud to be recognized by leading national and international healthcare organizations for our commitment to delivering exceptional services.

Branch Name: Colombo Central

Colombo Central is the flagship branch, renowned for its modern facilities and leading medical services. The branch is headed by Dr. Samantha Perera, our CEO, with Mr. Kasun Fernando as the Hospital Manager. Special services include 24/7 emergency care, advanced cardiac surgery, and a specialized oncology unit. This branch also offers cutting-edge diagnostic facilities and VIP suites. Colombo Central is known for its patient-centric approach and the ability to handle high-risk surgeries and complex treatments.

Branch Name: Kandy Medical Hub

Kandy Medical Hub is nestled in the scenic hills and provides comprehensive care to the Central Province. The branch is led by Mr. Dinesh Gunasekara as the Hospital Manager, with Dr. Nirmala Wijesinghe as the Chief Medical Officer. Services include orthopedic surgeries, geriatric care, and a dedicated maternity wing. Our CEO, Dr. Samantha Perera, frequently oversees operations. The hospital offers patients an incredible view of the Kandy Lake, along with world-class medical services and state-of-the-art operation theatres.

Branch Name: Jaffna Specialist Care

Jaffna Specialist Care is known for its expertise in treating chronic illnesses and rare diseases. Headed by Dr. Muthu Kuganathan, this branch offers specialized diabetic care, dialysis, and pediatric services. Mr. Ramesh Kumar, the Branch Manager, ensures smooth day-to-day operations. With a focus on community health, this branch offers outreach programs and free health screenings. Our CEO, Dr. Samantha Perera, frequently visits the Jaffna branch to personally oversee the medical advancements and patient care facilities.
